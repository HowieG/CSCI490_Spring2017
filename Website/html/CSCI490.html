Submissions

The following is a data manipulation tool for Los Angeles area schools that have offered a Computer Science-related course since 1988. Its purpose is to aid a study on the relationships between various school demographics and the availability of Computer Science at that school.

This project was done as part of CSCI 490: Directed Research, University of Southern California

Research was collected with help of the California Department of Education's online resources.
